THE BEAR OF GOOD TIDINGS
Jim Terr (c) 2019

It’s mis’ry and perfidy day after day. 
You’d like to stay hopeful but it seems there’s no way.
The lying and self-dealing, the cruelty and pain
Lay you out mournful again and again.

It’s easy to falter and give up for good
If we fail to remember as well as we could
That there’s hope. Cuz it’s overcast day after day
From the things that the worst of us think, do, and say.

CHORUS:
But the bear of good tidings, he sits on the shelf
Or the desk or the dresser, content in himself
That no matter how much bad news comes through the tube
There’s probably more good news that’s happening to you. 

There’s love being made and there’s love being shown, 
It’s a miracle how much each day we have grown. 
There’s so much that’s good and heroic and kind in us
And the bear of good tidings just sits there reminding us.
