About this page: Author Jim Terr is a singer/songwriter, actor, documentary and satirical video producer with over 2.5 million views on his YouTube channel, from Las Vegas, New Mexico USA. He is no fan of Donald Trump in his current form, nor demagogues and authoritarians in general.

In particular he is opposed to Trump’s efforts to intimidate and acquire other countries, until he demonstrates that he can contribute something positive in his own country. Terr opposed a similar effort in the previous Trump administration, to intimidate Qatar, with a song resulting in tens of thousands of video views on YouTube and Facebook, and press coverage from the Quatar press and Al-Jazeera.

He has a special fondness for Denmark, having visited a good friend there several times. He currently has a CD of his and fellow New Mexicans’ music on the German Bear Family label, and has produced a feature-length film featuring "outed spy" Valerie Plame. His work is supported partly via Patreon.com.

Om denne side: Forfatteren Jim Terr er en singer/songwriter, skuespiller, dokumentar- og satirisk videoproducent med over 2,5 millioner visninger på sin YouTube-kanal fra Las Vegas, New Mexico USA. Han er ingen fan af Donald Trump i sin nuværende form, heller ikke demagoger og autoritære generelt.

Især er han modstander af Trumps bestræbelser på at intimidere og erhverve andre lande, indtil han viser, at han kan bidrage med noget positivt i sit eget land. Terr modsatte sig en lignende indsats i den tidligere Trump-administration, for at skræmme Qatar, med en sang, der resulterede i titusindvis af videovisninger på YouTube og Facebook, og pressedækning fra Quatar-pressen og Al-Jazeera.

Han har en særlig forkærlighed for Danmark, da han flere gange har besøgt en god ven dér. Han har i øjeblikket en cd med sin og andre New Mexicans' musik på det tyske Bear Family-label, og han har produceret en langfilm med den "udledte spion" Valerie Plame. Hans arbejde støttes delvist via Patreon.com.

In nineteen hundred and thirty-three
Mr. Hitler made a bold decree
That will no doubt be repeated here,
Yes, friends, we have everything to fear.

See, their congress building burned on down,
All the way to that German ground
And the Hitler gang knew it would happen
And when it did their hands started clappin’.

Cuz it gave them an excuse to do
What they already were planning to:
To state, declare, announce and decree
A national emergency
That because this country is overrun
With subversive, commie verrrr-mun
We’ll take this opportunity
To cancel all your liberty.

Yes it pains us to do such an awful thing
But it’s the only way to let freedom ring,
Is to round up all those commie finks
And clean up everything that really stinks.

Yes we hoped it wouldn’t happen here
It’s the one thing that we truly feared,
But some fire, disaster, explosion or crash
Means we gotta clean up that commie trash.

And on such a sad, unfortunate note,
We’ve also gotta suspend the vote
Cuz y’all cannot think very clearly
When the rights that we hold oh so dearly
Are threatened by this emergency.
We must respond with urgency.
Well I think you probably get the idea:
We’re in trouble but we’re gonna save ya, heah?

So don’t be surprised in ‘26
When Donald’s gang is in a fix,
For some reason or other, for our own protection,
They find some excuse to cancel the election.

You heard it here first. (whistle...)
