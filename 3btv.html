13 “shorts” by Santa Fe producer featured on UK video website
         (11-22-04)

Thirteen short films by Santa Fe documentary and video producer Jim Terr will be spotlighted on the prestigious British streaming-video website, www.3btv.com , which will celebrate "Jim Terr Day” Tuesday, December 7.

Terr is one of the few non-British producers, and has the second-highest number of films, featured on the 3btv site. Although the “Jim Terr Day” showcase is December 7, the thirteen shorts are already posted for viewing on the site.

NOTE: Since "Jim Terr Day" has now passed, 3btv.com has all these shorts listed under LIBRARY and AUTHOR (Jim Terr).  Terr's featured pieces range from documentary and public safety spots to comedy shorts, four of which feature his character, ultra-patriotic c/w singing star “BuDDy.” (see list of shorts on below). One of the BuDDy videos, “The Passion of BuDDy,” will also screen at the Santa Fe Film Festival December 2 and 4, in the “New Mexico Sampler” program.

Several of the short films to be featured on 3btv.com have been shown in various New Mexico film festivals, and some are recently-produced. "Jim Terr Day" will be introduced by promotional spots featuring BuDDy, who also hosts a 50-minute episode of the UK-based country music program, The Internet Country Show ( www.InternetCountryShow.com ).

Terr is the producer of a series of critically-acclaimed educational documentaries recently released on a DVD entitled “Five Frightfully Fine Videos” (see www.BlueCanyonVideo.com ), which is also featured in one of the shorts posted on 3btv.com. Release parties for the DVD will be held Monday and Tuesday, December 6 and 7, in Albuquerque and Santa Fe.

Terr's bio on www.3btv.com states:

Jim Terr produces documentaries as well as comedy shorts, and (like everyone) hopes to produce features. A political animal, Terr sings and records many satires at www.BlueCanyonSatire.com . In a non-political vein, he and his friend "BuDDy" perform often.

A film and stage actor, he enjoys using actor friends in his short films, as well as bringing out the talent in "inexperienced" talent, creating custom short films for anyone who wants to be in a film and can pay for it!

About 3btv.com: www.3btv.com is the UK's premiere Internet TV show reel website for new and unsigned talent. Using streaming media technologies, 3btv offers showcases of films, animations, music video, comedy and sketches from independent producers, and is dedicated to showing films by new and unsigned artists. It was named “One of the top ten coolest sites of 2004” by Internet Video Magazine. [ 3btv press contact: Jeff Horne, jeff@3btv.com ].

Jim Terr shorts on www.3btv.com
          (descriptions as given on website) (11-22-04)

ALL IT TAKES Up-and-coming Hawaiian teen idol Lilia belts out this Jim Terr composition in her first, definitely exciting music video. The song is included on the “Demos and Diamonds” CD featured at www.bluecanyonproductions.com/cd.html . And the video is featured with many others on “The $2.98 Video Collection,” at www.bluecanyonproductions.com/$298.html .

BONE CRUSHER Traffic safety demonstration from the educational video, “NO BLOODSHED: The High Road to Understanding Driving Danger”, included in the new DVD compilation, “Five Frightfully Fine Videos.” ( www.bluecanyonvideo.com ) . Here, resurrected crash test dummy “Doofus Clueless” (Ryan Miera) demonstrates the tremendous force involved in even a low-speed auto crash. Edited by Paul Glickman.

GEORGIE GIRL Flash animation by Paul Glickman (www.ADDEnterprises.com), song written and performed by Jim Terr, parody of the 1967 Seekers' hit, “Georgy Girl.” For some reason, we expect US president George W. Bush will not be pleased by this.

I SERVED WITH GEORGE In response to the over $10,000 (US) in rewards being offered to find a witness to US President George W. Bush's “lost months” of military service in 1972, country-western singing star and American icon, the super-patriot “BuDDy” steps forward with his recollections. Even if they are complicated a bit by BuDDy's own emotional problems and agenda. Co-starring Paul Glickman , edited by Grant Taylor.

LAVERNE'S VIDEO REVIEW In one of her few for-video performances, the late and brilliant comedienne, Susan Jayne Weiss, of Santa Fe, New Mexico, USA, in her character as “Laverne Ganesh, Goddess in the Flesh,” introduces a performance video of a song by Santa Fe singer and video producer Jim Terr, called “Did It Hurt When You Fell Down from Heaven?” The song is included on the “Demos and Diamonds” CD featured at www.bluecanyonproductions.com/cd.html , and the video is featured with many others on “The $2.98 Video Collection,” at www.bluecanyonproductions.com/$298.html . Edited by Grant Taylor.

THE MAKING OF “FIVE FRIGHTFULLY FINE VIDEOS "Promo piece about the making of five low-budget but critcally-acclaimed educational videos released in the US over seven years, now compiled on one DVD (see www.bluecanyonvideo.com ). Shot in part by Paul Glickman , edited by Grant Taylor.

NARY A STITCH In this parody of the low-budget hit film, “The Blair Witch Project,” actor/producer Jim Terr discovers an even scarier secret lurking in the woods near Santa Fe, New Mexico, as he tracks down reclusive ex-movie star Tamar Tagwood. Co-starring Kent Crider and the beautiful Production Sisters [Beth Nommensen, Hadley Harper, Zoë Liepins]. This video is featured with many others on “The $2.98 Video Collection,” at www.bluecanyonproductions.com/$298.html . Produced by Jim Terr of Blue Canyon Productions in Santa Fe, New Mexico USA.

NEGATIVITY CENTRAL In case you don't get enough criticism in your life, here's a 900-number you can call to get all the abuse you'll ever need. Infomercial parody starring the late Susan Jayne Weiss, Eliot Stenzel and Jim Terr, edited by Brad Wolfley.

NEKKID Live performance by country-western singing star and American icon, the super-patriotic and politically incorrect “BuDDy,” of his stirring anthem of world peace and love. Co-starring Kerry Morrigan and Juli Ethridge of the Tricklock Company in Albuquerque, NM ( www.Tricklock.com ).

RUN BUDDY RUN In this parody of the German hit feature film, “Run Lola Run,” country-western singing star and American icon, the super-patriotic and politically incorrect “BuDDy,” barely misses his multi-million-dollar prize from the magazine publishers' representatives who come by his house at the most opportune time. Co-stars Ryan Miera, Yann Lussiez, Jennifer Lussiez and Beth Nommensen . Co-directed, shot and edited by Paul Glickman (www.ADDEnterprises.com ).

THE PASSION OF BUDDY Movie trailer parody featuring incredibly famous country-western singing star and American icon, the super-patriotic and politically incorrect “BuDDy.” Features excerpts of live performances and various shorts including “Run Buddy Run,” “Turner's Bison Burgers” and “NEKKID,” which are also featured on 3btv.com. Edited by Grant Taylor.

THE WOMAN WHO WON ROD STEWART'S HEART Starlets and supermodels have captivated rock's most famous playboy, but the woman who finally settled him down will surprise and enchant you as she tells how it happened. Starring Barbara Mayfield, edited by Christopher Wright.

TURNER'S BISON BURGERS Always looking for publicity, as well as to make his own selfless contribution, country-western singer and American icon “BuDDy” does a freebie commercial for American billionaire and buffalo-meat magnate Ted Turner. Shot by Beth Nommensen, edited by Grant Taylor.
