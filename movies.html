BE IN A MOVIE - The perfect gift! (see below)**

In addition to numerous highly-rated videos, CDs, books etc. (see Products Page) Blue Canyon Productions has three excellent feature-length films in development.

The first, a love-triangle-thriller, is the most exciting stage play we've ever seen, whose script we've "optioned" from the author.

This is an extraordinarily easy and inexpensive property to shoot (barring expensive big-name stars, which we think are generally unnecessary), because virtually all the action takes place on one set, in one room!

Like the other properties described here, this film requires funding in order to be produced, and we would be glad to discuss any serious investment proposals. Any of these films can be produced for under $200,000, plus cost of post-production (editing, etc.) and promotion.

We have two other original properties in development, one of which is a "mockumentary"* involving the character, "Buddy", featured in "Buddy's Old Fashioned Web Site" (click here).

We have some solid contacts in Hollywood as possibilities for "pick-up" of the completed films for distribution and promotion, which is a very important element in such ventures.

The latter two properties involve some of the same sort of music you'll hear at Buddy's Old Fashioned Web Site and on our internationally-acclaimed CD . These two movie projects also have a positive social element mixed in with the action—which is not particularly true of the love-triangle-thriller described at the top.

**These two movies also have guest roles for actors and actresses paying to be included—a great gift for the right person! We are confident of being able to write good custom-made speaking roles for almost anyone, and of directing them in a credible way so that the result is something everyone can be proud of, and which enhances the final film.

This is also a novel way to help finance these two productions, and to add an unusual publicity element which will actually help promote the film when released!

Please contact us for more information, and if you'd like to see a sample of our style of writing and production (albeit a very low-budget sample), please see "The $2.98 Video Collection".
