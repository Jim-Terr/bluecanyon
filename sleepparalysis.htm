SleepParalysis.us

Information on the SCARY but HARMLESS
and VERY COMMON phenomenon of
Sleep Paralysis.

You wake up (or you're almost asleep)
and you feel PARALYZED...

A frightening feeling, but extremely common.

Not only is this nothing to be afraid of, but it's
a fascinating, cross-cultural phenomenon, and
very revealing about the mechanisms of sleeping,
relaxing and dreaming.The explanation is surprisingly simple.


Please use this site to assure yourself of one thing:
Sleep Paralysis is common and (in the absence of other
sleep disorders) it is not harmful, though it can be
--and usually is -- quite frightening.
