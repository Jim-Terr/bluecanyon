Trump's Appeal: An educational project

A video documentary of interviews with Trump supporters
to help non-supporters understand his appeal



If you are interested in being interviewed for this project,
please see this video introduction, and read information below

 

I’m Jim Terr, and I’ll be shooting interviews with Trump supporters – hopefully around the country – to be compiled into a 60- or 80-minute program, consisting only of these videos, with no editorial comment or commentary.

My purpose is to get an idea directly from Trump supporters, what they like about the guy, what is the appeal. Because I - along with many others – do not get it. That’s right, I’m no fan of Mr. Trump, and I make no secret about it.

But, this being a serious documentary, done under foundation funding standards, it will be strictly exploratory, giving Trump fans a chance to express what the appeal is – personally, emotionally, policy-wise, whatever. And again, there will be no editorializing on my part, no trick questions or ambush questions, no editing other than for brevity or clarity.

I have done many well-received, foundation-funded educational videos – you can read about many of them at www.BlueCanyonVideo.com – and I will stick to those standards of objectivity. PLEASE NOTE that I have conducted MANY video interviews – from World War Two vets to Holocaust survivors, to “life” survivors, with fairness and with a gift for drawing people out.

All I ask of the interviewees is that you talk about Mr. Trump, what appeals to you personally about him, and avoid mention of Obama, Hillary, Liberals, etc., and all that comparitive stuff – we have plenty other places to discuss that – just stick with Trump’s appeal to YOU..


If we interview, you’ll be asked to sign a release specifying all this – including my promise to not distort or editorialize on what you say – and of course understanding that probably not all interviews will be used.

Finally, I would prefer that you not read a prepared text (notes are OK) – but rather speak from the heart please. And I’d prefer so-called “private citizens”, not any sort of spokesmen, party activists, consultants, bloggers, talk radio hosts, etc. If that’s you, please contact me at the email shown and we can try to arrange this.

First set of interviews will be Hawaii (Oahu and Kauai) in early January.
