July 16, 2002 Inside: Jump Forms | Mini Tips | Random Images/Links Form Based Jump Navigation To save space and to provide a nifty way of giving your visitors access to your pages, you could consider using a form-based, drop-down jump navigation system. This is practical especially if you have many sections to your site, with multiple pages in each section. Normally, you would have a series of links for each page, and more links underneath for all of the subpages. Using this Jump script, you could simply name the various sections, and place a form selector below each to show all the sub-pages, without taking all that space to show them.
