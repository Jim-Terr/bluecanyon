INFORMATION ON THE “BRILL BUILDING” MOVIE

As you may know, or have found out by now, I have been producing videos habitually for years, with almost 1,000 on YouTube alone. And writing songs by the thousands, probably, since the 1970s.

When I was asked to run in the US Presidential primary in Arizona in 2012, I saw a comedic opportunity for a feature film, and a friend and fan invested enough for me to do so. I procrastinated for a good seven years, though, during which time many elements and themes (and investors) were added, and the title changed from “A CHICKEN IN EVERY GARAGE” to “THE BRILL BUILDING MOVIE.” The Brill Building in NYC being where many top hits of the 60s were written.

When Valerie Plame agreed to be my “anchor woman” well over a year ago, I invested in a much better camera so that she and the remaining elements of the film could be shot in a much higher resolution, 4k, so that if this ever gets to The Big Screen, these new elements would look very sharp.

As listed on the reverse, no doubt with some omissions, many people supported me along the way, if not with this movie specifically, then just in general and in keeping my songwriting going. The 95+ “players” listed were wonderfully willing to “play along” since my video phase commenced in 1993.

Several foundations have also supported me in my work, in ways and themes which have found their way into this movie, and which I hope reflects our shared intentions.

By the time I got around to editing (a couple months ago), the many years of transferring old video files etc. to new computer drives really caught up with me, as every little video file I wanted to use in this project was hidden in an unknown folder, making that part of the job especially tedious. Also numerous breakdowns and deficiencies in hardware and software needed to be addressed along the way.

One of the things I hope to do if this movie takes off, or sells to a distributor with resources, is to take the time to go back and dig out video files closer to the original source, and therefore with a better look – no small task – and hire a more skillful editor. For now, I hope the idea comes across and that you enjoy the film. I look forward to screenings in Albuquerque, Las Vegas NM, and wherever invited.

My larger hope for the project is that it will generate support and investment for more movies and more songwriting and recording (I’ve got many songs yet to record and pitch, written and unwritten), with resources to distribute and “monetize.” As reporter Steve Terrell said in a somewhat unexpected “preview” of the film a couple weeks ago, I have a “black belt in self-promotion” and I make no apology for that. If I don’t promote me, who will? Though I wish I had done it much more wisely in previous decades.


How much of this movie is factual? Well, most of it. I won’t break down the true and untrue elements, but obviously I’ve added a few fanciful details to the story line – though not many.

Again, I’ve been blessed with so many talented and generous friends, and I thank you for your support, and all of you for sharing this debut moment. Onward! On a final note, I’ve brought along some copies of various CDs and my book tonight, for those interested. And many of the videos excerpted in the movie tonight are on Facebook and YouTube.

Jim Terr bluecanyon2 -at- juno.com June 3, 2019
