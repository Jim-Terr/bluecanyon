E-mail response to NM Lt. Gov and declared Gubernatorial candidate Diane Denish, in response to her fundraising e-mail below...

 

I’m very aware that you’re running for Governor, because I keep reading about the incessant fundraisers. Do you suppose you’ve got enough money in the bank, enough of a lead in fundraising, to consider how rotten is this system of private, competitive, constant fundraising?

I don’t plan to vote for any candidate (even my favorite Democratic and liberal ones) who doesn’t stop and think and take a stand for public campaign financing. I would like a little representation here too, and would like to see you-all have a little time for study, contemplation, governing, and looking out for MY interests – which you must know is inhibited when you have to spend ALL your time fundraising. And it would be cheaper for all of us that way, as you must know.

So please take me off your list, and let me know if and when you decide to speak out for public campaign financing. I’m not just picking on you (although you’re in the best position to take the lead on this) – I’ve “editorialized” on this frequently.  This is 2007 already – let’s get with it. Best wishes.

P.S. My name is not James.
