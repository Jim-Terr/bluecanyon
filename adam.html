A STORY: I once met, at some event or other at the Lensic Theater in Santa Fe, a ~70-yr-old couple from Lubbock, TX, middle-American-looking as can be.

In the course of conversation, they menitoned that they were active in their local chapter of PFLAG (Parents and Friends of Lesbians and Gays). I asked if they had a gay son or daughter. They (one of them) answered, "No, it's just a justice issue with us."

That made a big impression on me..
