MOTOR CITY SWING
                        © 2009 Jim Terr

Well the Detroit River’s wide and strong.
It just keeps on swingin’, rollin’ along,
Sayin’ “Hey, Detroit, are you still there?
Been havin’ a kinda quiet year.”

The iron ain’t rollin’ like it used to
But we’re down not out, bright not blue,
We still know how to rock all night,
Fly so high and hold on tight.

Maybe pump out buses and trains instead,
   (or: "Maybe pump out trains and buses too"..)
All kinda crazy things.
Whatever it takes, that’s what you make
To make the Motor City swing!

 

Renaissance Center, Woodward Ave,
All them good things we still have.
D.I.A. and ol’ Wayne State,
We’re still jumpin’, pick you up at eight!

So let them presses stamp and stomp,

Til we bounce on back, get on the track
