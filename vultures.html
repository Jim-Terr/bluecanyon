 "The Vultures of Vegas," is a video adaptation of the best-selling book,
 "Wildest of the Wild West," by the late historian Howard Bryan.
The book and the film document the contention of Bryan and other historians that
early Las Vegas, NM, "surpassed Tombstone, Deadwood, and Dodge City in violence."
